# How it plays

Formation hold. Triple-stacking Lawn Knight (3-star) puts an absurdly tanky wall on the
frontline; Barbarians 3 + Suburban 3 proximity armor stack multiplicatively on the same
unit, making it nearly unkillable while it stays near its bin. Fence Posts clump mid-
formation (infantry cohesion bonus), charging their rage quickly to slow anything that
breaks through. Eave Watcher sits at maximum range and picks off low-HP enemies with the
bypass bolt — crucial for closing rounds fast before attrition catches up.

Placement matters more here than any other comp: bins bunched close together so the
Suburban 3 proximity bonus covers as much of the formation as possible.

**Win condition:** outlast everything. Superior sustain means the turtle wins any long
fight — it just needs to not lose before both 3-thresholds come online.
**Loses to:** Park Lockpickers — fast flankers with bin-snipe damage slip around the wall
and assassinate the spawners before the armor bonus kicks in.
