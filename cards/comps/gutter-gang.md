# How it plays

Pure numbers comp. Every bin is City + Farmers, so 7 bins activates both 3-thresholds
simultaneously. Farmers 3 triples spawn output; City 3 then rewards the resulting mob
with escalating attack rate.

Rabble (×3, effectively 3-star) is the backbone — cheap, plentiful, and the first thing
you're buying. Alley Dasher fills once you have a second trait slot open; its vault-behind
rage puts a fast flanker in the bin-melt position. Dumpster Hog comes online last as the
lid-slam anchor that clears space for the swarm to refill.

**Win condition:** opponent drowns in bodies before they can deal enough AoE to thin the herd.
**Loses to:** Coastal splash — Coastal 3 AoE on hit punishes exactly the density this comp
needs to function. One Coastal Tinkerer lineup with range advantage can farm the clump.
