# How it plays

Ranged control. Buzzwire (×3, 3-star) is the primary damage dealer — long-range archers
with an arcing bolt rage that chains to adjacent targets. Barnacle holds the line and
creates a slow field in melee range (anchor-slam + Tinkerer 2 slow on rage hits). Undertow
flanks on the opposite side, threading its wave-pass through the enemy formation and
leaving a slow wake for Buzzwires to clean up.

With Tinkerers 3 active, every rage attack gains extra range — Buzzwire's arc reaches
further, Undertow's pass gets a deeper wake. Coastal 3 splash on auto-attacks punishes
any opponent who tries to swarm to close the range gap.

**Win condition:** keep enemies at arm's length and disassemble them with layered slows
and splash while they can't reach the archers.
**Loses to:** Park Lockpickers / Park Cavalry — speed + stealth units can close the
range gap before the slow field is established, and the comp has low raw HP to absorb
a burst flanker.
